## Methodology

Keywords were selected by balancing **search volume** (how often people search the term), **relevance** (how well it describes HourFlow), and **competition** (how many established apps target this keyword). We prioritize niche trade/contractor terms where big players (Toggl, Harvest, FreshBooks) have less presence.

**Important:** Apple's algorithm indexes the App Name, Subtitle, and Keyword field together. Words already present in the name ("HourFlow", "Time", "Invoice") or subtitle ("Track", "Hours", "Send", "Invoices") do NOT need to be repeated in the keyword field â€” Apple indexes them automatically.
